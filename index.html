Lucknow
City of Nawabs

Lucknow, city, capital of Uttar Pradesh state, northern India. It is located roughly in the centre of the state on the Gomati River, about 45 miles (72 km) northeast of Kanpur. It is the city of Nawabs. It is famous for their historical places like Imambara, Bhool Bhulaiyaa, Satkhanda, Residency and more.
